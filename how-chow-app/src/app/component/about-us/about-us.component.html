We Are

HowChow is your number one trusted food review app. At HowChow, we know that food reviews can sometimes be deceiving, and so for the past few weeks, a group of highly motivated* individuals gathered together to solve this most prominent problem in the bustling metropolis of Morgantown.

At HowChow our goals are:

    Allow customers to trawl for the best dishes in town. Give customers other consumers' insights, by allowing them to view previous reviews of dishes around town. Let customers upload reviews and photos of their favorite dishes.

At HowChow our mission is:

    We strive to provide our customers with the best service available in the industry. We aim to increase customer satisfaction and loyalty by providing the fastest and most accurate service.
    WE -- ARE HOWCHOW.
